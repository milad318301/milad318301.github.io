Play The Game Now

Help Guide for downlading the game on PC

  1. Download Origin from the official website.
  2. Log in with your EA account, or create a new one.
  3. Hover to the "Browse Games" tab on the left side of the app and select Apex Legends > Apex Legends.
  4. Click Add to Library.
  5. Click Download with Origin.

Help Guide downloading the game on Playstation

  1. Turn on the PlayStation 4 system and launch the PlayStation Store from the home screen.
  2. Highlight search, then type in "Apex Legends." The game may also be listed in other store categories.
  3. Select the game, then select "Download" or "Add to library."

Help Guid downloading the game on Xbox

  1. Step 1: Open The Microsoft Store. From your Xbox One's dashboard, launch the Microsoft Store.
  2. Step 2: Search For "Apex Legends" Using the Search Bar, look for "Apex Legends".
  3. Step 3: Select And Download The Game.
